Research Statement

I currently do research on Mechanism Design, and my research interest spans the fields in (the intersection of) Operations Research, EconCS and (Cyber-)Security.

Particularly, I have a special interest in mechanism design for blockchain and decentralized AI, and my recent research focus is on the design of incentive-secure decentralized trustworthy AI platforms, for a general scope of reliability and social sustainability in AI and economic platforms, including (particularly) the current concern and initiative of AI safety for the upcoming AGI era.

My research has won Best Paper Award at NeurIPS Workshop on Decentralization and Trustworthy Machine Learning in Web3.

While I am currently enjoying my research projects and am optimistic in the long-term value of the innovations in my research topics, I am aware of the intense competitions of the academic job market and recognize that I would need to enhance my publication records with more fruitful and solid research works before attempting to obtaining a faculty or postdoc position. Therefore, I anticipate extending my graduation timeline based on my research progress, allowing me to focus on my research with a calm and dedicated mindset, ultimately preparing me for future post-graduation opportunities.
