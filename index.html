Bio

I am in Tsinghua University, but the alias "wiku30" dates back to days of OI in senior high as an MO contestant. It means "winged kuriboh", a sprite card in the old-fashioned card game Yu-Gi-Oh. More impressively, it is a symbol of those bygone years when I began to develop my interest in computer science, along with those unusual experiences in both groups.

Life has changed rapidly as time flies by, but it also witnesses the growth of me as well as those fellows. In the Information Era, there are great deals of challenges to keep pace with, while the initial dreams are still well worth sticking to.

Now I am in IIIS50, a class with excellent students around me. Struggles as there are, I feel it as the dreamland I have always looked forward to. In the busy life here, I wish that I would gain more enchanting knowledge and improve myself towards a new level!
